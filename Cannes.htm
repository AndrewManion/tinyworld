Dock Walking and Cabin Work.

I was lucky to get a great ride from Chamonix to the French Riviera from a ski guide who was heading home. We talked the whole way down the mountain and to the sea until he let me out in Nice. I was overjoyed to splash sea water on my face and I spent the evening in a small French apartment with rain falling outside. In the morning, I took a train to Cannes. As we wrapped down the coast, I hastily drew boat diagrams and wrote boating terms on my hand. As soon as I stepped on board in Port Pierre Canto I got right to work on Brigitte's ship, Nikita. The next thing I knew I was steering the ship out of the harbour and into the open waters of the Mediterranean.
