Family Time in France.

We all arrived in Chamonix valley. Mother, Pops, Aunt Cathy, John, Bryn and myself. We started the week with fine French food, mostly cheese, and family fun on the mountain. We explored Le Tour and Brevent all together and then John and I took a day to conquer the infamous Grand Montet. We even passed under mighty Mont Blanc to get to Courmayeur on the Italian side.
