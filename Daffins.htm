Alpine Farming

After fleeing Paris I frantically caught trains across France and through Switzerland. Bruce the guitarist gave me a ride up the hill to the Unterberg and then I startled Jona, one of the other volunteers, as I knocked on the glass window of the house. I settled in and early the next morning we volunteers gathered around the table with our pouradge and waited for instruction from Marcus. The next two weeks included good work of clearing rocks, building a dead hedge, destroying blackberry bushes, and installing turbines in mountain streams. It also included learning to bake sour dough bread, presentations around the fire, and trips to nearby Feldkirch.
