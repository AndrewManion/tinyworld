On mountain roads with Gus

Angus and I met in Innsbruck to commence a new adventure. We were prepared with skis and low-rated sleeping bags. On the final leg of our journey I doubted the bus we were on and made a hasty decision to get off. This left us stranded in no-man’s-land for an hour but it did also allow for time to change into warmer clothes for the night trek ahead. We eventually found a camp for ourselves at the trunk of a tall, dead tree, a short hike along Nordic ski trails to get back to the spa. The next morning we skiid out and made our way into the resort.
