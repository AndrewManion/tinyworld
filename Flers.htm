Working in the Rain

The ferry arrived in the dead of night on the shores of Normandy. I took a few busses and trains to arrive at the small town of Flers. I ate croque monsieur and waited for Jennifer, my new host, to pick me up. The next days I battled emotions, tree limbs and shivering dogs who had turned on one of their own. When the weather changed I was able to bike through the valleys of Suisse Normandy. To close out my time, my host family invited me to join for Passover dinner. After supper I exchanged mind games with the children and the next day I was off to say goodbye to Tere in the city of love.
