How does the landscape effect how you perceive your home?
The answer to that is quite easy. It’s not so much the landscape as the people. The landscape almost doesn’t come into it. And it’s also… I’m trying to think of the right way to phrase it so that it can be easily put. It’s the changeability of the landscape. It’s like the forest. You would think that the forest will be a steady environment, but it's not this far north. You know, it can be soaking with rain one day, absolutely soaked. Everything is wet as hell, and the next day the sun can come out. It's a bit like Siberia. That's a similar climate. The sun can shine for only an hour, half an hour, and everything is bone dry. So, it goes from one extreme to the other, and that has a very big effect on the psyche and on your health. It's, it's a very unsteady place to live, and it's very harsh on a human being. It’s not the same in all microclimates. It’s how it is on most of the area here and in this microclimate.
