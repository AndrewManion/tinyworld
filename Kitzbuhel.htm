Chicken Boys

After several laps of the sella ronda, around the tall granite thumbs, we took off towards Kitzbuhel. We arrived, made introductions and we’re shown our main duty at the hostel: feed the chickens. We fit in a sprint around Salzburg’s old castles and the next days we skiid anywhere but on the piste. Fresh tracks were made in snow which had fallen the previous week but the majority was soft all the same. Gustav took off back home to the Netherlands and I took another day to climb the mountain on ski.
