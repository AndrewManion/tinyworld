Nairobi and the Farm That Feeds It

When the pilot woke me up on the floor of the airport in Muscat, I didn't know where I was going. When we landed in Kenya, it made more sense. We came in over the mara in the afternoon sun. Just as quickly as we landed, I got myself to the outskirts of Nairobi, near Kibuku, to a camping tent set to overlook the forest farm. My real job, however, was to be apprentice to Esbon, the master carpenter.
