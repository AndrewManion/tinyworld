Building in Accord with Nature

The route from Patagonia to the Chilean coast brought me through the vineyards of Mendoza, atop the Andes mountains, up, down and around the emerald hills of Valpo, and finally to Udo’s home in Laguna Verde. I arrived in the trunk of Udo’s jeep and found Hendrik chopping wood. We gathered around the table and ate palta for dinner that night and every other as the wood burning oven heated the clay walls around us and kept our toast warm.
