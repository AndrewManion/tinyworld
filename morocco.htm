Hitchhiking towards the Sahara

I arrived in Morocco without much of a plan. I'd heard from the Chamonix ski guide that hitchiking could be effective so I walked past the cabs outside the airport and made my way to the beach. In no time I was in a car heading to Taghazout. I spent weeks in the waves until I decided to go out and see the rest of the country. I hitched all the way to the sahara and then up to the strait.
