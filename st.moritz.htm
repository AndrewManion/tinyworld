Shivering and Sliding.

After the weekend in Munich Tere was off on a flight tour and I headed back to the mountains. Corvatsch and Corviglia were calling. I set up my tent behind the chapel after digging out a pile of snow. For five days I skid across the two mountain areas and spent my evenings eating pizza in grand hotels. One day I had a close call in the cliffs and spent hours hiking out of it.
